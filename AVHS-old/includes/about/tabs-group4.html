Request Transcript

To request an official transcript, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the Transcript Request Form available in the Counseling Office
  2. Provide valid photo identification
  3. Include the complete address where the transcript should be sent
  4. Allow 5-10 business days for processing
  5. There is a $5.00 fee for each official transcript

For current students, transcripts can be requested through the Counseling Office. Alumni should contact the Registrar's Office.

Transcript Types

Official Transcript

Sealed transcript with school seal and signature. Required for college applications, employment verification, and official purposes.

Fee: $5.00

Unofficial Transcript

Student copy for personal use. Not suitable for official purposes but useful for planning and reference.

Fee: Free

Electronic Transcript

Secure digital delivery to participating institutions. Faster delivery and reduced risk of loss.

Fee: $5.00

Rush Transcript

Expedited processing for urgent requests. Available for an additional fee.

Fee: $15.00

Processing Time

Standard Processing

5-10 business days from the date of request

Most common request type

Rush Processing

2-3 business days from the date of request

Additional fee applies

Electronic Delivery

1-2 business days for processing + instant delivery

Available to participating institutions

Important Notes

  • Processing times may be extended during peak periods (college application season)
  • Incomplete requests will delay processing
  • Payment must be received before processing begins
  • Transcripts are mailed via USPS First Class Mail unless otherwise specified
