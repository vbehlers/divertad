About

Welcome to Antelope Valley High School, home of the Lopes! We've been a cornerstone of the Lancaster community since 1912, and we're proud of our deep roots in the Antelope Valley. For over a hundred years, we've been dedicated to providing a top-notch education in a place that feels like a home away from home.

Here at AVHS, we're more than just a school: we're a family. We believe in building strong relationships and creating a place where every student can discover their passions and potential. Our teachers are dedicated to helping students succeed, not just in the classroom, but in life. We're all about rolling up our sleeves and working together to help our students become creative, critical thinkers, and responsible citizens.

Mission and Vision

Our Mission

Antelope Valley High School provides an academically rigorous learning environment that promotes creativity and builds meaningful relationships in order to develop complex critical thinking skills necessary to be successful in the 21st century.

Our Vision

Students at Antelope Valley High School recognize their capacity to contribute to their community and are prepared to pursue a clear post secondary plan developed throughout high school.
