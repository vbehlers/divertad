About Our School

Welcome to Antelope Valley High School, where excellence meets opportunity! Our school has been a cornerstone of the Lancaster community for over a century, providing students with the tools they need to succeed in an ever-changing world.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower every student to achieve their full potential through rigorous academics, meaningful relationships, and real-world experiences.

Academic Excellence

Our academic program is designed to challenge and inspire students at every level. From foundational courses to advanced placement opportunities, we provide a comprehensive education that prepares students for college and career success.

Core Academic Areas

  • English Language Arts: Comprehensive reading, writing, and communication skills
  • Mathematics: From Algebra to Calculus, including AP courses
  • Science: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Environmental Science
  • Social Studies: World History, US History, Government, and Economics
  • World Languages: Spanish, French, and American Sign Language

Advanced Placement Program

We offer over 25 Advanced Placement courses, allowing students to earn college credit while still in high school. Our AP program has consistently achieved above-average pass rates, with many students earning college credit in multiple subjects.

Student Activities & Athletics

Beyond the classroom, we offer a wide range of activities that help students develop leadership skills, build friendships, and discover their passions.

Sports & Athletics

Our athletic program includes 15 varsity sports, with teams competing at the highest levels in our league. Recent achievements include league championships in football, basketball, and track & field.

Clubs & Organizations

Students can choose from over 30 clubs and organizations, including:

  • National Honor Society
  • Student Government
  • Robotics Club
  • Environmental Club
  • Art Club
  • Music & Drama
